Automatic sync between your PC and phone (optional). Get a free account at jsonbin.io → open API Keys → copy your Master Key and paste it below on this device, then Save. Every change you make here now saves to the cloud automatically a couple of seconds after you type — no button to press. On your other device (phone or PC), open the app, paste the same Master Key, then copy the Bin ID shown here into that device's Settings too, and Save. From then on both devices silently stay in sync: whichever has the newest change wins, checked every time the app opens. The Push/Pull buttons in the ⋯ menu still work if you ever want to force a sync manually.
